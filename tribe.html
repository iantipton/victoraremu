The Tribe Project - Empowering Communities Through Connection

Tribe is a powerful and user-friendly app designed to connect volunteers and support providers with those in need, fostering a strong sense of community. Whether youâ€™re offering your skills or seeking assistance, Tribe makes it easy to find, connect, and manage support with just a few taps.

For those seeking help, the app streamlines the process of finding local volunteers and support providers, ensuring a quick and seamless experience. Users can also securely make payments and manage their support, giving them full control over their wellbeing.

Key Features

  • Seamless Volunteer & Support Matching - Easily find and connect with local volunteers or support providers.
  • Effortless Support Management - Organize ongoing or one-time assistance with an intuitive interface.
  • Secure Payments - Integrated Stripe payment processing for safe and reliable transactions.
  • Real-Time Notifications - Stay updated on requests, responses, and transactions.

Technologies Used

  • Kotlin - Modern, concise, and efficient development for Android.
  • AndroidX - Flexible UI design for a smooth user experience.
  • Push Notifications - Real-time updates to keep users informed.
  • Apollo - Efficient GraphQL API communication.
  • Stripe - Secure and seamless payment processing..

Developing Tribe allowed me to combine my expertise in Kotlin and GraphQL with a mission-driven project that brings real value to communities. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, Tribe provides a seamless and empowering experience for users looking to give or receive support.
