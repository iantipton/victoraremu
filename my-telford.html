My Telford - Report Issues, Improve Your Community

A fast and simple way for local residents to report problems and help keep Telford clean, green, and safe. Whether it’s a pothole, litter, or any other issue, you can get in touch with the right people in just a few taps—no waiting on the phone or waiting for offices to open.

Simply follow the on-screen instructions, attach a photo if needed, and submit your report using your phone’s GPS so it can be located easily. My Telford App takes care of the rest!

Key Features

  • Quick Issue Reporting: Report problems in your local area from anywhere, at any time.
  • Attach Photos: Provide a clear picture of the issue to speed up resolution.
  • GPS Integration: Automatically pinpoint the location of the reported problem.
  • Simple and Efficient: No more phone queues or long waiting times—just tap and send.

Technologies Used

  • AndroidX - Ensures a responsive and flexible user interface.
  • Material Design - Provides a modern, intuitive, and consistent look and feel.
  • Push Notifications - Keeps users updated on the status of their reported issues.
  • Android Room - Manages local data storage and retrieval efficiently.
  • Microsoft Identity - Secures the app with Active Directory authentication.

Developing My Telford App allowed me to tackle real-world challenges in community reporting and municipal management. By leveraging the above technologies, I was able to deliver an easy-to-use and reliable tool that encourages civic engagement and streamlines problem resolution.
